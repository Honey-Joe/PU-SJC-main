About the Program

A Pioneering Collaboration

St. Joseph's College Society and Pondicherry Central University jointly offer high quality MBA Twinning programme with specialization in Marketing, finance. HRM, International Business and General for all degree holders including office goers. The marked difference between the conventional distance education programme and this Twinning programme is that the students of twinning programme would undergo a serious extended class room interactive sessions handled by including industry experts on Sundays. Next to Loyola College (Chennai), St. Joseph's College is the only college in Tamilnadu authorized by the Pondicherry University to conduct the Twinning Programme.
