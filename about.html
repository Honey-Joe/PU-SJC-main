About the Programme

Programme Concept

The Twinning Programme is an innovative educational concept which widens the scope of educational opportunity and access for students. The twinning concept envisages a tie-up between Directorate of Distance Education, Pondicherry University and Society of St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli.

The Management programmes already offered by Pondicherry University through Distance Mode are now offered at St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli. This twinning concept gives a gamut of choices to the student community to learn and assimilate the basics of the subjects through well-designed and meticulously executed academic Programmes.

Pondicherry University

Pondicherry University (PU) is an outstanding institution of higher education and is one of the Central Universities in India. Teaching, Research, Extension and Outreach are the primary functions as seen in all Central Universities.

  • • Has affiliated Colleges in Puducherry, Karaikal, Mahe, Yanam and Andaman & Nicobar Islands
  • • Member of Association of Indian Universities and Association of Common Wealth Universities
  • • Over 250 faculty members and 2,200 students in campus programmes

The Twinning Institutions

St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli

The host institution for MBA twinning programme, is an affiliated first grade College of Bharathidasan University. Established in 1844 by the Jesuit Fathers of Society of Jesus.

  • • Celebrated its sesquicentenary in 1995
  • • Five Stars status by NAAC in 2000
  • • Recognized by UGC as College with Potential for Excellence
  • • NAAC A++ accreditation in 2019
  • • Awarded Special Heritage status by UGC in 2015
  • • NIRF 25th Rank (2023)
  • • 181 Years of Educational Excellence
